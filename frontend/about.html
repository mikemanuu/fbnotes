About fbnotes

fbnotes is a lightweight tool designed to help you save, organize, and revisit your favorite Facebook content; posts, notes, and bookmarks, all in one simple dashboard.

Built for creators, learners, and everyday users who want to collect ideas and inspirations without losing track, fbnotes ensures your data stays private and secure. You can access it from any device, anytime.
